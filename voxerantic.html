Voxerantic

Performance Date: Saturday, August 16

Voxerantic is a four-person indie rock group from Chicago, Ill. The band members are Howie Larson, Jason Aldridge, Ruby Crewe, and George Chapman. The band formed in 2007 and released their first album Prodigal Dog in 2009. Their sophomore album Brunch for Four released to critical acclaim in 2010 and earned them their first Grammy nomination for Best Band Rock Performance.

Visit their website: www.voxerantic.com
